The Core Problem

Knowledge today lives in slides, inboxes, and individual market teams.

Takeda's platforms are systems of record and engagement — they capture that an interaction, inquiry, case, or submission happened. They do not systematically ask: What did we learn? Is it reusable? Where does it apply next? How safely can it transfer across borders? With multiple market-level launch moments looming across three assets, the cost of not reusing learning compounds — duplicated MLR content, repeated analyses, re-discovered payer objections, and slow launch sequencing.

The differentiation narrative
The FDA's Sept-2021 boxed warnings for JAK inhibitors (cardiac events, malignancy) created a category fear. Selective TYK2 inhibitors like deucravacitinib carry no black-box warning — and zasocitinib's ~1.7-million-fold selectivity is the core scientific story. The launch challenge isn't efficacy; it's educating HCPs on "is TYK2 safer than JAK?" consistently across every market.

Why zaso is structurally invisible

As an unapproved compound, zasocitinib lacks the canonical entities AI engines cite — no Drugs.com monograph, GoodRx page, FDA label or WebMD article. Competitors own these. Takeda's content clusters in trade press (FierceBiotech, AJMC) and journals (JAMA Dermatology) — not the patient-facing comparison engines that LLMs read. A study of 130,070 US health queries found brand-and-location modifiers were less likely to trigger AI Overviews; engines favor authoritative, independent sources.

Module 6 · Regulatory, Compliance & AI Governance

Aggressive on intelligence. Conservative on trust.

Every recommendation is source-lineage-shown, confidence-scored, MLR-status-aware and human-approved. Generative AI is kept out of regulated decisions and used only for governed, human-approved learning surfacing — per EU AI Act, GMP Annex 22 (draft), 21 CFR Part 11 and Takeda's own ethical-AI framework.

What travels

Aggregated, de-identified patterns ("X% drop off at benefits verification"). Governance metadata, confidence, MLR status, source lineage, allowed-use. Market-applicability scores. Human-approved recommendations.

What never travels

Patient-level / special-category data across borders. Off-label or pre-approval promotional claims. Ungoverned generative output in regulated decisions. Raw records of any kind — only patterns abstracted from them.
